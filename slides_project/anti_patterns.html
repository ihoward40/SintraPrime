Four Anti-Patterns

That Kill Autonomous Systems

Scope Creep
Adding features without proving the core loop works first. Leads to a bloated, unmaintainable codebase that fails silently.
Remedy: Prove value before expanding.
Vibes-Based Decisions
"It feels faster" or "I think it's working." Without metrics (P95, Error Rate), you are flying blind and will crash.
Remedy: Trust only receipts & metrics.
No Rollback Plan
Deploying changes without a tested way to revert. When production breaks, you are trapped fixing forward under pressure.
Remedy: Always have an undo button.
Silent Failures
Swallowing errors or ignoring exit codes. The system looks healthy but is actually doing nothing or leaking data.
Remedy: Fail loud. Fail fast.
