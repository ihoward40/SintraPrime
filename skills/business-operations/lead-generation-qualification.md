# Lead Generation & Qualification Skill

**Category:** Business Operations  
**Agent:** Lead Generation & Qualification Agent  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Active  

## Purpose

Multi-channel lead capture, verification, and automated outreach to build pipeline.

## Capabilities

- Web scraping for prospect identification
- Email verification and deliverability checking
- Personalized cold outreach sequence generation
- Lead scoring based on engagement and fit criteria
- CRM integration and pipeline management
- Multi-channel tracking (TikTok, web, email, referral)

## Lead Scoring Criteria

| Dimension | Weight | Signals |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Fit Score | 30% | Company size, revenue, industry |
| Intent Score | 25% | Technology stack, job postings |
| Behavior Score | 20% | Content engagement, site visits |
| Qualification Score | 15% | Budget authority, decision-maker |
| Urgency Score | 10% | Timeline indicators, trigger events |

## ROI Impact

- 3x pipeline increase vs manual prospecting
- Replaces 2-3 SDRs ($150K-$225K annual savings)
- 40% higher conversion rate with personalized outreach
- Supports Founders Program signups and Product Hunt launch
