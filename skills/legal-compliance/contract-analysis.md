# Contract Analysis & Redlining Skill

**Category:** Legal Compliance  
**Agent:** Contract Analysis Agent  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Active  

## Purpose

AI-powered contract review that identifies risks, suggests edits, and ensures compliance with company playbooks.

## Capabilities

- Full contract parsing and clause extraction
- Risk scoring for each clause (low/medium/high/critical)
- Automatic redlining with suggested alternative language
- Compliance checking against regulatory requirements
- Playbook enforcement for standard terms
- Side-by-side comparison of contract versions

## Supported Contract Types

- Service agreements and SaaS contracts
- Employment agreements and NDAs
- Vendor and supplier agreements
- Trust documents and fiduciary agreements
- Licensing agreements (SintraPrime IP)
- Partnership and joint venture agreements

## Risk Scoring Framework

| Risk Level | Description | Action |
|------------|-------------|--------|
| Low | Standard market terms | Accept |
| Medium | Non-standard but acceptable | Flag for review |
| High | Unfavorable terms | Redline required |
| Critical | Unacceptable / illegal terms | Reject |

## ROI Impact

- Reduces legal review time by 80%
- Prevents costly compliance errors (avg cost of missed clause: $50,000+)
- Enables non-lawyers to handle routine contract review
- Saves $500-$2,000 per contract in legal fees
