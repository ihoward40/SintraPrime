# TCPA/FDCPA Violation Tracking Skill

**Category:** Legal Compliance  
**Agent:** TCPA/FDCPA Violation Tracker Agent  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Active  

## Purpose

Log and track illegal robocalls and debt collector harassment for statutory damage claims. This is a direct revenue-generating skill.

## Capabilities

- Robocall logging with timestamp, caller ID, and recording metadata
- FDCPA violation categorization (harassment, false representation, unfair practices)
- TCPA violation tracking (autodialer calls without consent)
- Statutory damage calculation ($500-$1,500 per violation)
- Court-ready evidence package generation
- Integration with STEVEN Legal Evidence Agent

## Violation Categories Tracked

| Violation | Statute | Damages |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Calls before 8am or after 9pm | FDCPA Section 806 | $1,000 |
| Calls to workplace after notice | FDCPA Section 805 | $1,000 |
| Threats of violence or prosecution | FDCPA Section 806 | $1,000 |
| False representation of debt amount | FDCPA Section 807 | $1,000 |
| Robocalls without prior express consent | TCPA Section 227 | $500-$1,500 |
| Calls after cease and desist letter | FDCPA Section 805 | $1,000 |

## ROI Impact

- $500-$1,500 per TCPA violation (statutory damages)
- $1,000 per FDCPA violation (statutory damages)
- Class action potential for systematic violations
- Average settlement: $3,000-$10,000 per case
