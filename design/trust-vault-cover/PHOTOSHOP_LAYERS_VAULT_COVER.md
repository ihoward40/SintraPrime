# Photoshop / Affinity / Figma Layers — Trust Vault Cover

This is a layer-by-layer breakdown matching the Canva template. Use it when you want strict control of blend modes, foil textures, and print polish.

## Document
- Letter 8.5"×11" @ 300 DPI, bleed 0.125"

## Layer Stack (Top → Bottom)

1) **FX: Dust Specks**
- Small gold specks brush
- Color: `#BA9B57`
- Opacity: 10–12%
- Blend: Screen

2) **FX: Edge Gilding**
- Soft vertical gradients on left/right edges
- Color: `#C6A861`
- Opacity: 8–12%
- Blend: Soft Light

3) **Footer Group**
- Divider line (1–2 px)
- Footer title + subline

4) **Lower Banner Group**
- Banner rectangle (black)
- Opacity: 10–15%
- Text block (3 lines)

5) **Sigil Group**
- Outer ring (stroke 6 px)
- Inner ring (stroke 2 px)
- Laurel vector (gold)
- Monogram: `ITH` or `HTN`
- FX (applied to group):
  - Bevel & Emboss: very subtle
  - Inner Shadow: low
  - Outer Glow: `#BA9B57` at 10% opacity

6) **Title Group**
- Primary title
- Divider ornament line
- Subtitle line 1
- Subtitle line 2 (tracking +100)

7) **Background FX: Gold Aura**
- Radial gradient circle
- Color: `#BA9B57`
- Opacity: 12–18%
- Gaussian blur: strong

8) **Background Texture**
- Black marble / vellum grain
- Opacity: 10–15%
- Blend: Overlay or Multiply

9) **Background Base**
- Solid black `#000000`

## Export
- Print PDF with bleed + crop marks
- PNG (2×) for digital cover/title-card
