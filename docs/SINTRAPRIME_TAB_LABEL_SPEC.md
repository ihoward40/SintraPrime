# SINTRAPRIME PHYSICAL TAB LABEL SPEC

Paper
- White or light gray cardstock; no colors, icons, or logos

Font
- Preferred: Helvetica Neue; Fallback: Arial
- Weight: Bold (not Black, not Regular)
- Size: 12 pt standard tabs; 11 pt if space-constrained
- Case: ALL CAPS

Alignment
- Left-aligned on the tab; vertically centered

Canonical Tab Text
- TAB 0 — ORIENTATION
- TAB 1 — [SUBJECT]
- TAB 2 — [SUBJECT]

Do not include
- “EXHIBIT”; emojis; decorative separators; dates

Spacing Rule
- Leave at least 3 mm margin on all edges of tab text; tabs step cleanly, not staggered randomly

Rationale
- Mirrors government binder norms; familiarity signals trust.
