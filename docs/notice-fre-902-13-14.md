# Rule 902(13)/(14) Evidentiary Notice (Draft)

**Title:**  
**Notice of Intent to Offer Certified Electronic Evidence Under FRE 902(13) and 902(14)**

**Body (verbatim-ready):**

> PLEASE TAKE NOTICE that the undersigned intends to offer at hearing and/or trial certain electronically stored information (“ESI”) as **self-authenticating evidence** pursuant to **Federal Rules of Evidence 902(13) and 902(14)**.
>
> The evidence consists of machine-generated records produced in the ordinary course of operation of a computerized evidence system (“Sintra Evidence Engine”), including:
>
> • execution manifests
> • append-only ledgers
> • cryptographic hash maps
> • verification outputs
>
> Authentication will be established through a **sworn certification of a qualified record custodian** (Exhibit 0), attesting that:
>
> 1. The records were generated automatically by a reliable electronic process;
> 2. The records were stored in a manner that prevents undetected alteration; and
> 3. The integrity of the records can be verified independently through cryptographic hash comparison.
>
> Verification artifacts are included as **Exhibit B**, enabling any party to confirm integrity without reliance on subjective testimony.
>
> No live witness is required for authentication.
> Any objection must be made within the time provided by rule.
