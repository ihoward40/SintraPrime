# Adaptation Matrix (Family Court / Civil / Administrative)

## Family Court

### What to emphasize

- Neutral, automated process
- No testimony from children or parties
- No credibility disputes

### What to avoid

- Technical jargon
- “Blockchain” language
- Over-engineering

### Framing

> “These records were generated automatically and verified independently. No one is asking the court to rely on anyone’s memory.”

---

## Civil Court

### What to emphasize

- Business-records analogy
- Regularly conducted automated activity
- Verification eliminates alteration risk

### Framing

> “This is no different from server logs or transaction records—only more verifiable.”

---

## Administrative Hearings

### What to emphasize

- Reliability over formality
- Efficiency
- Reduced need for live witnesses

### Framing

> “The agency can verify these records independently without live testimony.”

### Common admin finding

> “Sufficient indicia of reliability.”
