# Poppy AI Workflows — Creator Machine v1

Save each section below as a **named workflow** in Poppy AI.

---

## Workflow 1: Short-Form Script Engine

**Input:** raw idea OR raw voice transcript

**Output (must include):**
- 1 hook (single line)
- 5–7 short lines (spoken cadence)
- 1 CTA (single line)

**Workflow prompt:**
Write a short-form script.

Constraints:
- Write in my natural voice: clear, confident, direct.
- No filler, no corporate tone, no hedging.
- Optimize for retention.
- Keep sentences punchy and readable on-screen.

Return in this exact format:

HOOK:
<one line>

SCRIPT (5–7 lines):
1) <line>
2) <line>
3) <line>
4) <line>
5) <line>
6) <line optional>
7) <line optional>

CTA:
<one line>

## Workflow 2: Long-Form Authority Builder

**Input:** same idea

**Output (must include):**
- outline
- full script or article
- section headers

**Workflow prompt:**
Turn this idea into a long-form authority piece.

Constraints:
- Write in my natural voice: direct, specific, practical.
- No fluff. No motivational filler.
- Make claims only when supported by clear reasoning.
- Use section headers that are short and punchy.

Return in this exact format:

TITLE:
<1 title>

OUTLINE:
- <section 1>
- <section 2>
- <section 3>
- <section 4>

FULL DRAFT:
<draft with headers>

## Workflow 3: Monetization Translator

**Input:** any finished content (draft or published)

**Output (must include):**
- what problem it solves
- who it’s for
- 3 offer ideas
- 1 soft CTA

**Workflow prompt:**
Analyze the content and translate it into monetization.

Constraints:
- Be concrete and practical.
- Offers must be simple: one outcome, one deliverable, one price range.
- Keep it ethical and non-icky.

Return in this exact format:

PROBLEM THIS SOLVES:
- <1–3 bullets>

WHO IT'S FOR:
- <1–3 bullets>

3 OFFER IDEAS:
1) Name: <offer>
   Type: <Service|Product|Bundle>
   Outcome: <single sentence>
   Deliverables: <1–3 bullets>
   Price range: <$X–$Y>

2) Name: <offer>
   Type: <Service|Product|Bundle>
   Outcome: <single sentence>
   Deliverables: <1–3 bullets>
   Price range: <$X–$Y>

3) Name: <offer>
   Type: <Service|Product|Bundle>
   Outcome: <single sentence>
   Deliverables: <1–3 bullets>
   Price range: <$X–$Y>

SOFT CTA (one line):
<one line>
