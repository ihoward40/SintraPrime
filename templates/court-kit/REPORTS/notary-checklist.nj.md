# NOTARY CHECKLIST (NEWARK, NJ)

Purpose: notarized acknowledgment page for the transcript hash declaration (Door 2).

Bring to the notary:

- Government-issued photo ID for the signer.
- Printed `verification-transcript.pdf` (or the exact filing set, if applicable).
- Printed “Hash Declaration (Exhibit Page)” (generated values filled in).
- Printed “Notary Acknowledgment (New Jersey — Acknowledgment)” page.
- A pen.

Rules:

- Do not sign the acknowledgment page until you are physically in front of the notary.
- The notary completes the acknowledgment and applies seal/stamp as required.
- If the transcript is re-generated, re-hash and regenerate the hash declaration page.
