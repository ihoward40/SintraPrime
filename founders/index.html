The Platform

What is SintraPrime?

SintraPrime is a multi-branch governance operating system for autonomous AI agents. Every action is receipted, every decision is auditable, and every workflow is fail-closed by default.

  • 🛡
    Governed Agents Howard Trust Navigator, SentinelGuard, and specialized agents operate under constitutional governance.
  • ⚖
    Court-Ready Evidence SHA-256 chained, Ed25519-signed receipts produce court-admissible audit trails.
  • 🔗
    Multi-Platform Adapters for Gmail, Notion, Slack, Discord, Telegram, WhatsApp, and more.
  • ⚙
    Workflow Engine YAML/JSON declarative automation with Validator, Planner, and Executor pipeline.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Founder's Program.

What exactly is the Founder's Program?
The Founder's Program is an exclusive early-access membership for the first 50 users of SintraPrime. Founders make a one-time investment starting at $2,500 and get full platform access, direct communication with the creator (Isiah Howard), Founder's Council access, priority feature requests, and lifetime pricing that never increases — even as the platform grows and public pricing goes up. Use code EARLYBIRD at checkout for 20% off (limited to the first 10 founders).
What does "lifetime pricing" mean?
Your one-time founder investment locks in lifetime access at your tier level forever. As the platform grows and pricing increases for new members, your founder benefits and access remain unchanged. Individual Founders invest $2,500, Team Founders invest $7,500, and Enterprise Founders invest $15,000 — all one-time payments with no recurring fees for your founder-tier benefits.
Do I need technical experience to use SintraPrime?
SintraPrime is designed for both technical and non-technical users. The governance dashboard and agent workflows use a visual interface. For advanced use cases, you can write custom YAML/JSON configurations. Founders also receive white-glove onboarding to get you set up regardless of your technical background.
What industries is SintraPrime designed for?
SintraPrime is particularly powerful for trust administration, legal compliance, financial services, and any industry where AI actions need to be auditable and court-admissible. However, the governed agent framework is general-purpose and works for any workflow automation use case.
Can I cancel my membership?
Yes, you can cancel at any time with no penalties. However, if you cancel and later wish to rejoin, you will be subject to the current public pricing at that time — your founder rate cannot be reinstated.
How do I get support?
Founders have access to a private Discord channel, weekly office hours with Isiah Howard, and priority email support. Architect and Visionary tier members also receive 1-on-1 sessions and dedicated support channels.
